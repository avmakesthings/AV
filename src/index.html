UX Research

I focus on improving products and services through better user experiences. I collect and analyze user data and feedback through standard UX methodologies, which enables me to clearly define user needs.

UX Design

I help my clients create memorable user experiences by showing them what motivates their users. This knowledge helps me to define product direction and help build solutions that put the user first.

UI|Ix Design

My passion for human centered design and design background allow me to produce visually stunning user interfaces and user centered interaction flows that define my clients’ brand and product identity.

Prototyping

I use rapid prototyping to visualize and develop my ideas and to engage my clients through the design development process. I build prototypes in both low and high fidelity using techniques that mirror real world applications.

Hi there!

My name is Anastasia Victor and I’m a user experience researcher and interaction designer. I’m most interested in the way new technologies can affect the way we interact with physical and virtual environments, though my work broadly encompasses other areas like digital fabrication, living architecture and … toilets.

I like thinking about and working on projects that embrace innovative and sustainable technologies and have broader social and environmental implications. I engage in problem solving through research and both physical and digital prototyping.

I have a Master’s degree in Architecture from the University of California, Berkeley and am currently doing freelance UX research and UI/UX design with some futuristic, freelance architectural design too. It’s pretty awesome.

Check out some of the work I’ve done over the last few years, most of which is collaborative. You can also find me on linkedin, pinterest, twitter and issuu.
