Pplanter

A rapidly deployable public urinal and sink, that uses modular biofilters to treat urine and wastewater on-site. It’s the answer to at least some of your urban sanitation needs…

Brainwave

An investigation into the possibilities of using Brain Computer Interfacing as an input modality for automated environments.

Secret Scoop

A website redesign that aimed to both update the functionality of former site for a San Francisco food start-up and provide a more user friendly interface.

PHree Loo

A low-tech toilet prototype that uses the ‘safe sludge’ methodology to inactivate pathogens in the sanitation value chain, for the safe and sustainable management of human waste.
